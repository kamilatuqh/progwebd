Information System, Sepuluh Nopember Institute of Technology

Information System Department Institut Teknologi Sepuluh Nopember (ITS) students will learn about information system integration, development, and management, business process modeling. Students will also learn to be creative and innovative, leadership, and teamwork. This department has a mission to give qualified education development and applied system.This Department provides 37 lectures, which divided into five laboratories as their expertise, 9 lecture for MSI laboratory, 8 lectures for SE laboratory, 6 lectures for IKTI laboratory, 8 lectures for RDIB laboratory, 6 lecture for ADDI laboratory.Their students also have the achievement, such as The Best Poster in ASEAN-EU STI Days for the section: smart cities & ICT systems for rural areas di Hanoi-Vietnam 2016, The Best participant in 20 ASEAN junior research from the technology management intensive course (support by AUN/SEED-Net JICA) 2016.In 2009, this department won the award as The Majors With The Most Cooperation and affects graduates who are easy to get a job or become an entrepreneur. Job opportunities for information system graduates are IT operation staff, lecture, Business Analyst, System Analyst, Application Developer, and etc.
